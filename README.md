#### 1. AI-Driven Market Intelligence & Agentic Decision Support System
**Role:** Senior Generative AI Engineer &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Duration:** Jan 2025 – Present  
**Stack:** Python, OpenAI APIs, LangChain, RAG, FastAPI, AWS, Vector DBs, BERT

**The Problem:** Financial analysts were drowning in fragmented market signals — structured price data, Twitter sentiment, and news feeds — with no unified intelligence layer to generate actionable recommendations automatically.

**What I Built:** An agentic AI platform where LLM-driven agents autonomously ingest structured and unstructured market data, reason over historical signals using RAG, and generate risk assessments and recommendations. Fine-tuned transformer-based sentiment classifiers (BERT/LLM) process high-volume real-time data streams. All capabilities are exposed via production-grade FastAPI services.

**Impact:** Replaced hours of manual analyst work with real-time, explainable AI-driven decision support. Established evaluation frameworks using golden datasets and continuous prompt testing.

#### 2. Product Recommendation & Conversational Intelligence Platform
**Role:** Senior AI Engineer &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Duration:** Dec 2022 – Sep 2024  
**Stack:** Python, FastAPI, LLM APIs, Vector Embeddings, AWS

**The Problem:** A large e-commerce platform needed personalized, context-aware product discovery beyond basic collaborative filtering — one that understood *why* a customer wanted something, not just what they bought before.

**What I Built:** Combined traditional ML recommendation models with LLM-powered contextual reasoning. Built retrieval-augmented pipelines matching user intent to products via behavioral and contextual vector embeddings. Designed conversational product discovery workflows using function-calling patterns.

**Impact:** Improved user engagement and conversion rates through real-time, personalized recommendations. System deployed at scale on AWS with full monitoring and reliability controls.

#### 3. Customer Retention Intelligence Platform

**Stack:** Python, NLP, LangChain, LLM APIs, Agentic AI, Vector DBs, AWS

**What I Built:** An AI-assisted decision-making system combining NLP analysis of customer communications with LLM-powered churn reasoning agents. #### 4. Automated Article Intelligence & Content Digitization Platform
**Role:** Senior Data Scientist &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Duration:** Jan 2019 – Jun 2020  
**Stack:** Python, Computer Vision, OCR, NLP, TensorFlow, OpenCV, spaCy, AWS

**The Problem:** A US-based legal media publisher managing 35+ regional newspapers relied on ~300 offshore resources to manually digitize and archive printed newspaper content.

**What I Built:** An end-to-end ML document processing pipeline — page segmentation, article detection, OCR-driven text extraction (Tesseract), NLP-based classification, metadata tagging, and content indexing. **Impact:** Reduced manual review workforce from ~300 resources to fewer than 10 reviewers. Enabled faster archival, searchability, and digital publishing at scale.
