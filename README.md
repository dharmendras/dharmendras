Built an AI-powered market intelligence platform that combines:

* Multi-agent reasoning workflows

* News and social sentiment analysis

### Business Challenge

Financial analysts were spending hours manually reviewing:

* Market data
* News feeds
* Social media sentiment
* Risk indicators

The process was slow, inconsistent, and difficult to scale.

### Solution

1. Collect market signals

4. Evaluate risk factors
